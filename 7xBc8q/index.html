Tokyo Skytree

東京スカイツリー

Went in April 2, 2022 during my family's stay in Japan.

The 3rd tallest structure in the world, it has a futuristic design, and, due to its seismic proofing, it's pretty resistant to earthquakes; cushoning them so you won't even feel a thing. It also has really nice lights that go on at night! The design of the two illuminations, Iki, meaning chic, stylish and Miyabi, elegance, refinement, are traditional Japanese aesthetic concepts.

Tokyo Skytree attracts people from around the world because of its beauty and height.
